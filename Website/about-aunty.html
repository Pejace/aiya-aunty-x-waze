ABOUT AUNTY?

malaysia is full of aunties. you see them at the markets, you see them on public transport, and if you’re lucky, you hear them before you even spot them.

AUNTY

means to the malaysians

'aunty' it is not just used for the family members but it can be use for every middle-aged women (40s - 60s) even someone you doesn't know. bear in mind, it is very abrupt to call ladies who are in their late 30s aunty, it is an indication that they are old.

leng lui

as a malaysian, we highly recommend foreigners to use the term 'lenglui' (beauty) to call all women, it is very effective to get their respond and even receive a discount!
