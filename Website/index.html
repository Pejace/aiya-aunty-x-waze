aunty talking PATTERNS

THE ART OF AUNTY LINGGUE

ah boy

young boy

use "ah boy" to indicate male that is younger than you; same for ah girl. for example: ah boy ah ~ you better behave yourself ah!

aiyor

oh my

use 'aiyor' when you hear something that upsets you. the longer it is, the more you are expressing your consolations. " aiyooooooorrrrrr"

walau

gosh

use 'walau' to express your dissatisfactions, you can add 'eh' behind it to emphasize how unpleasant your feelings are. "walau eh! liddat also cannot!"

aiya

opps

'aiya' was being used whenever a mistake was done. for example: "aiya~ i forgot to bring my umbrella."
